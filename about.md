Josh is full stack software engineer with a passion for building products that enable significant impact for end-users. His passions include finance, working on motorcycles, pretending to play golf, and collecting wine. 

Josh's career did not start out in software engineering, but he always had a knack for tinkering with things. His entry into software engineering began by way of a bootcamp called [General Assembly](https://generalassemb.ly/). He has done software engineering stints at The Desire Company (fka desirelist), Fitch Solutions, and is currently at Amazon Web Services. In the spirit of working backwards, Josh has been working on his Master's in Computer Science at Georgia Tech (OMSCS) and is expected to complete this in 2025.

Spaces that are interesting to Josh include blockchain, web3, fintech, and social impact.
